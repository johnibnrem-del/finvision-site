We speak like a sharp, trusted friend — not a bank, not a startup pitch deck. Short sentences. Active voice. We trust the reader's intelligence and never over-explain.

10 — Don'ts

Common Mistakes

Rules matter most when paired with their violations. The following breaks the system and will make the brand feel inconsistent or amateurish.

✗ Wrong gradient direction
FinVision
Never change the gradient direction. Always 135deg only.
✓ Correct gradient
FinVision
Primary gradient: 135deg · violet → indigo → sky.
✗ Gradient text everywhere
Snap a photo. AI reads it. You see results.
Gradient text loses impact when overused. Max 1–2 words per heading.
✓ One gradient word
Your money. Clarified.
White text carries the sentence. Gradient marks the key word only.
✗ Light background
FinVision on white
FinVision is a dark-first brand. Light themes break visual identity entirely.
✓ Dark background only
FinVision on #09090b
Always #09090b base. Only exception: doc-mode or legal print.
✗ Full-opacity brand colors as text
This heading
Solid violet on dark backgrounds lacks contrast. Use gradient text or white.
✓ Gradient or white text
This heading
Gradient text on dark: readable, beautiful, on-brand.
